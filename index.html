Mission:
The purpose of the chapter shall be to foster, promote, maintain, and recognize scholarship among the students of Santa Monica College; to develop programs offering cultural, social, or enrichment experiences; to promote service to the college and community; and to emphasize the development of leadership, character, and judgment.
How we work:
  • Join
    Attend the first meeting of the semester and make sure that you've fulfilled all your necessary requirements to be a general member. (See constitution for further details)
  • Help
    Attend our weekly meetings, give back to your community and get your hours in! Make sure to sign up for events and follow rules carefully to be a proper AGS SMC member.
  • Grow
    Grow not only as a member, but as an individual striving to reach excellence while helping others throughout your journey.
  • Organizers
    If you'd like to request service from AGS SMC, speak to our Events Secretary to plan out the logistics according to your event.
