INDEPENDENT HOURS
In order to earn transcript notation, you must complete 10 of your 20 volunteer hours through AGS sponsored events. However, you have the opportunity to earn the rest through approved, independent volunteering opportunities. Here is a list of approved volunteering opportunities. If you would like to add something to this list, please stop by one of our board meetings. We meet every Tuesday from 11:15 AM to 12:35 PM in MC 66. Please note that we typically only approve volunteering opportunities that are accessible to all SMC students. After the service has been approved, please make sure you download the Independent Hours form from the Resources section of our website. You MUST keep track of your hours with your supervisor and submit the Independent Hours form no later than December 4!
**Please make sure to contact the independent event beforehand to ensure that the event is still accepting volunteers**
